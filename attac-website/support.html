Common Questions

Question: How do I register? 
Answer: Go To www.tw-attac.com and click on 'Purchase'. Then click on 'Click here to use our Secure Server' Once you have purchase ATTAC, I will automatically receive email notification and request from you four (4) Tradewars Handles. Once I receive those from you, I will send you the registration codes for those handles. 
Question: Is there an ATTACK/CAPTURE script? 
Answer: No, use the Capture Calculator in ATTAC->Calculators->Ship Capture 
Question: Does ZTM save the data if it crashes or I lose my connection? 
Answer: Yes, since ZTM saves the data as it scrolls across the screen in real-time. Therefore, you only need to start off from where you last left off
